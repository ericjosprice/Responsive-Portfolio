About Me

Driven technologist with nearly a decade's worth of managerial experience and a passion for problem-solving. I have a B.A. in Mathematics from The University of Texas at Austin. I worked for four years at an electronic medical records software company as a Strategic Account Manager, where I gained a high-level perspective of the operations at hundreds of medical clinics and the software industry.

In the summer of 2019, I completed an immersive coding bootcamp where I gained proficiency with the following technologies: JavaScript, jQuery, Git, Node.js, React.js, Express.js, MySQL 5, Mongo DB, Laravel, and cloud application development. I am currently pursuing a career as a Software Engineer so that I may utilize my skills, experience, and desire to make tools that solve problems for everyone.
